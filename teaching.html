Teaching

Not unlike my research, my teaching experience has been equally as diverse and interdisciplinary with courses ranging from artificial intelligence to music perception and cognition.

In my lessons, I value communicating accurate and honest information, fostering an exciting and engaging atmosphere, providing challenging material and expectations combined with the freedom to make mistakes and the courage to take chances, and ensuring students feel confident in the knowledge they have acquired.
